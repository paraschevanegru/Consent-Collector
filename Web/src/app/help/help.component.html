How to register?

First step

Click on Create an account to go to the register form.

Second step

Fill in all the inputs with your information.The Register button is disabled until you fill in all the inputs.

Final step

Click on Register button. Your account has been created and you will be redirected to the Login page.

How to Login?

Fill in the inputs with your username and password. Then click on the Login button

How to recover your password?

First step

Click on Forgot your password? to go to the recover page.

Second step

Fill in the inputs with your email and phone number. Click on the Verify button to verify the data that you filled in.

Final step

Fill in the inputs with your new password. Click on the Submit button and you will pe redirected to the Login page./p>
